Where it all began

A single bite
changed everything.

It was the summer of 2018. Nikul Goyani bit into a freshly picked Alphonso mango in a Ratnagiri orchard and realised something — no store-bought juice had ever come close to this.

Thick, aromatic, impossibly golden. The kind of flavour that disappears the moment manufacturers touch it with heat, dilution, or chemistry. That afternoon, Raw Pressery was born — with one obsessive goal: bottle this, unchanged.

The Ratnagiri belt in Maharashtra carries a Geographical Indication (GI) tag — the same protection given to Champagne and Darjeeling tea. It's not just a mango. It's a place, a season, a microclimate. And we guard it fiercely.

How we do it

The Cold-Press
Difference

Heat kills enzymes, strips aromatics, and flattens flavour. We press at 4°C so the mango arrives in your bottle the way it left the tree.

01
Hand-Picked at Peak Ripeness
Every Alphonso is harvested by hand during the 6-week Ratnagiri season — not before, not after. No compromises on timing, ever.
02
Cold-Pressed at 4°C
Our hydraulic presses extract every drop of juice at ultra-low temperatures, preserving natural enzymes, vitamins, and the full aromatic signature of the fruit.
03
Zero Additives. Bottled Pure.
No sugar. No preservatives. No concentrate. No water. Just mango — exactly as nature made it — sealed within 90 minutes of pressing.

What We Stand For

🌱
Radical Purity
If nature didn't put it in the mango, we don't put it in the bottle. No exceptions, no negotiations, no "natural flavourings".
🤝
Farmer First
We pay Ratnagiri farmers 40% above market rate and commit to 5-year procurement agreements so they can plan, invest, and thrive.
🔬
Science of Freshness
Cold-pressing at 4°C preserves enzymes that pasteurisation destroys. We obsess over the science so you get the nutrition.
♻️
Zero Waste Orchards
Mango skins and pulp are composted back into the orchards. Our packaging uses 80% recycled materials. We close the loop.
🌍
Fair Global Pricing
Our geo-aware pricing ensures a customer in Mumbai and one in London both pay a fair local equivalent — never inflated for export.
❤️
Relentless Honesty
Every ingredient is disclosed. Every sourcing decision is published. If we ever compromise our standards, you'll hear it from us first.
